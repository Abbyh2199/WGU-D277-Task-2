Welcome to the Sunshine State

Florida is a state located in the southeastern region of the United States. With a population of over 21 million, Florida is the third-most populous and the 22nd-most extensive of the 50 United States. The state is bordered to the west by the Gulf of Mexico, to the northwest by Alabama, to the north by Georgia, to the east by the Atlantic Ocean, and to the south by the Straits of Florida. The state's capital is Tallahassee and its most populous municipality is Jacksonville. The Miami metropolitan area, with a population of almost 6.2 million, is the most populous urban area in Florida and the seventh-most populous in the United States. Other urban areas in the state with a population of more than one million are Tampa Bay, Orlando, and Jacksonville.

Florida's Economy

Florida's economy relies heavily on tourism. Florida is the top travel destination in the world. The warm climate, beautiful beaches, and world-famous theme parks attract millions of tourists every year. The state is also a major producer of citrus fruits, especially oranges. Florida produces more than 70% of the oranges consumed in the United States. Other important industries in Florida include aerospace, agriculture, and international trade.
